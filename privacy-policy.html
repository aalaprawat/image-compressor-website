Overview

Image Compressor: MB to KB is designed to help you reduce image file sizes on your Android device. The main compression workflow runs on your device. Your selected images are not uploaded to our server for compression.

Images and On-Device Processing

When you choose an image, the app reads that image locally, creates a compressed version, lets you preview the result, and saves the compressed copy if you choose to save it. Compression does not require internet access.

The app may temporarily store compressed preview files in the app cache while you are working. Saved images are written to your device gallery or Pictures folder when you choose to save them.

Information We Collect

The current codebase includes Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may collect app usage events, device and app information, approximate location derived from IP address, crash diagnostics, performance-related crash data, and other diagnostic information provided by Firebase. The app code uses sanitized event values such as size buckets, reduction percentage buckets, screen events, and error categories instead of uploading your actual photos or filenames.

We do not use Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics to upload the images you select for compression.

Permissions

Depending on your Android version, the app may request access to storage or the gallery so you can select images and save compressed copies. Older Android versions may require read or write external storage permissions. Newer Android versions may use system media pickers or scoped storage behavior.

Third-Party Services

The current codebase includes Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services are provided by Google and may process diagnostic or usage information according to Google's policies.

No advertising SDK was detected in the current codebase. The website does not include tracking scripts or analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Diagnostic and analytics information may be processed by Firebase as described above. Your selected images are not shared with our server for compression.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Data Security

We design the compression workflow so your images stay on your device. No app can guarantee perfect security, but avoiding image uploads for compression reduces exposure of your personal photos.
