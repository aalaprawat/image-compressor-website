FAQ

Quick answers before you install.

Does the app upload my images?

No. Compression happens on your device. Your images are not uploaded to our server for compression.

Does compression need internet?

No. Image compression works offline.

Will my original image be deleted?

No. The app saves a compressed copy and does not delete the original unless a future option clearly asks and you choose it.

Can I control image quality?

Yes. Manual compression mode lets you adjust quality and size settings.

Is the app beginner-friendly?

Yes. It is designed for everyone, with clear choices and simple steps.
